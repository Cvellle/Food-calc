# Meals & Nutrients Tracker

<span className="block text-lg text-gray-600 mt-4">
  Track meals, understand ingredients, and evaluate nutrient intake against
  daily or custom ranges.
</span>

---

## How it works

<div className="p-2 grid gap-6 mt-6 md:grid-cols-3">
  <div className="rounded-xl border p-5">
    <h3 className="font-semibold text-lg">1. Build meals</h3>
    <div className="text-sm text-gray-600 mt-2">
      Meals are composed of multiple ingredients with precise quantities.
    </div>
  </div>

<div className="rounded-xl border p-5">
  <h3 className="font-semibold text-lg">2. Aggregate nutrients</h3>
  <div className="text-sm text-gray-600 mt-2">
    Each ingredient contributes nutrients which are summed per meal or per day.
  </div>
</div>

  <div className="rounded-xl border p-5">
    <h3 className="font-semibold text-lg">3. Evaluate ranges</h3>
    <div className="text-sm text-gray-600 mt-2">
      Intake is compared against recommended ranges to detect excess or deficiency.
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
